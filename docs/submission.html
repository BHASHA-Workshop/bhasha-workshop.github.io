Instructions

Submitted papers must be neither previously published nor under review by another workshop, conference or journal.

Only electronic submissions in PDF format will be accepted. Submitted articles must be written in English, conforming to the page limit, in 10-point font and must render without error using standard PDF viewing tools.

Page Limit

Long Paper 8 pages
Short Paper 4 pages

Papers should use the standard submission template and instructions for ACL Rolling Review, available at Submission Template . The template is also available at Overleaf

Submission
To submit your paper, please follow below link to the submission site:

Direct Submission to BHASHA at OpenReview.net

Note
All ARR reviewed papers which have received a meta review before October 30th, 2025 may also be committed to the BHASHA Workshop.

Note that at least one of the authors of each paper accepted for presentation in 1st BHASHA Workshop at IJCNLP-AACL 2025 must be registered. All papers presented at the workshop will be included in the Association for Computational Linguistics Anthology.
